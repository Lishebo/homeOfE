About HOME OF URGENCE

HOME OF URGENCE was founded at the premise of 2015 under the provisions made available in CAP389 of the laws of Zambia, regarding the registration on business names.

We therefore specialize in;

  • food staffs

Our scope of trade stretches to diverse lengths as we are an open business who appreciates that opportunities are presented from different sections therefore we have identified areas in which we realize we can effectively and efficiently deliver with ease.
